## Project Overview

The **MWECAU Digital Voting System** is an online platform that replaces traditional paper-based student elections. Students can now vote securely from anywhere in the world during election periods.

### Why This System Matters

- **No more waiting in long lines** at voting stations
- **Students studying abroad** can participate in elections
- **Results available instantly** - no manual vote counting
- **Safe and transparent** - every vote is protected
- **Works across all MWECAU campuses**

### Problem We Solved

Before this system:
- Students waited hours in voting queues
- Students abroad couldn't vote
- Manual counting led to delays and errors
- Limited transparency in election process

After implementing our system:
- Voting takes less than 5 minutes
- Anyone with internet can vote
- Results available immediately
- Complete transparency and audit trails

## How It Works

```
Step 1: Open System → Step 2: Enter Reg Number → Step 3: Verify Details → 
Step 4: Select Candidates → Step 5: Submit Vote → Step 6: Get Confirmation
```

### Simple Process
1. Student visits the voting website
2. Enters their registration number
3. System shows their personal information
4. Student confirms their details are correct
5. Selects their home state for regional elections
6. Provides email address for updates
7. Gets access to voting dashboard
8. Views all available elections
9. Makes voting choices
10. Receives confirmation that vote was recorded
11. Can view results after election ends

## Security Features

Our system protects every vote with multiple security layers:

✅ **Secure Connection** - All data travels safely over encrypted internet  
✅ **One Vote Per Person** - System prevents duplicate voting  
✅ **Complete Records** - Every action is logged for transparency  
✅ **Permission-Based Access** - Users see only what they're allowed to  
✅ **Anonymous Voting** - Your vote choice remains completely secret  
✅ **Protected Data Storage** - All information is safely stored

## Election Types

### 1. Presidential Elections
- **Who Can Vote:** All MWECAU students
- **Purpose:** Elect university-wide student leadership
- **Scope:** Entire university

### 2. State Leadership Elections
- **Who Can Vote:** Students from the same home state/region
- **Purpose:** Elect representatives for regional student issues
- **Scope:** State/regional groups

### 3. Course Leadership Elections
- **Who Can Vote:** Students in the same academic program
- **Purpose:** Elect class representatives and course leaders
- **Scope:** Academic programs and courses

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I vote?
Visit the election website, enter your registration number, verify your details, and select your candidates. The process takes less than 5 minutes.

### Is my vote secret?
Yes, absolutely. The system ensures complete voter anonymity. No one can see how you voted.

### Can I vote from another country?
Yes! As long as you have internet access, you can vote from anywhere in the world.

### Can I change my vote after submitting?
No, votes are final once submitted. This protects election integrity.

### What if I have technical problems?
Support staff are available during all election periods to help with any issues.

### How do I know my vote was counted?
You'll receive immediate confirmation when your vote is submitted successfully.
