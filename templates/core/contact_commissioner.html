Frequently Asked Questions

Candidacy applications are managed by the Election Commissioner. Contact the commissioner directly to express your interest in running for a position.

All elections have a specific timeframe during which voting is allowed. If you anticipate being unable to vote during this period, contact the commissioner in advance to discuss possible arrangements.

Election results are calculated based on a simple majority system. The candidate with the most votes for a particular position wins. Results are verified by the Election Commissioner before being published.
