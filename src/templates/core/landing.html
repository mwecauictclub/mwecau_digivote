Voting Guide

How to Vote

  • Step 1 - Verify Eligibility: Only registered and verified students can participate in elections
  • Step 2 - Account Setup: Create your account with your registration number and email address
  • Step 3 - Review Candidates: Learn about the candidates running for various positions
  • Step 4 - Cast Your Vote: Vote for your preferred candidates during election periods
  • Step 5 - View Results: See real-time election results and candidate performance

Security & Transparency

  • Secure Authentication: Two-step verification ensures only eligible voters can participate
  • Anonymous Voting: Your vote is completely confidential and cannot be traced back to you
  • Audit Trail: All voting activities are logged and monitored for transparency
  • Real-time Results: Live vote counting with instant result publication
  • No Duplicate Voting: The system prevents any voter from voting multiple times

Election Levels

  • President Election: University-wide elections for student government president
  • State Leaders: Regional representatives from different states/regions
  • Course Representatives: Leaders representing specific academic programs

About This Platform

Democratic Elections for Student Government

The MWECAUSO Digital Voting System is a modern, secure platform designed to facilitate fair and transparent student government elections at Mwenge Catholic University.

Our mission is to make the voting process accessible, secure, and transparent for all eligible students while maintaining the highest standards of election integrity.

Key Features

  • Secure authentication system
  • Anonymous voting mechanism
  • Real-time result tracking
  • Multi-level election support
  • Fraud prevention measures
  • Comprehensive audit trails
