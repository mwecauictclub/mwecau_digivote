# MWECAU Election System - Project Overview
**Developed by MWECAU ICT Club**

---

## What is This Project?

The MWECAU Election System is a **modern digital voting platform** that replaces traditional paper-based elections at Mwenge Catholic University. Think of it as bringing democracy into the digital age - allowing students to vote securely from anywhere, anytime during election periods.

### Why This Matters
- **No more long queues** at polling stations
- **Students abroad** (like exchange programs) can still vote
- **Instant results** - no manual counting
- **Transparent and secure** - every vote counts and is protected
- **MWECAU campus support** - comprehensive coverage for all students

---

## How It Works (Simple Version)

```
Student → Registers with Reg Number → Verifies Details → Votes → Results Published
```

### Three Types of Elections:
1. **Presidential** - University-wide leadership (All MWECAU students)
2. **Residential Area Leadership** - Based on your housing area
3. **Course Leadership** - Based on your academic program

## Security Features (What Keeps Votes Safe)

✓ **Secure Connection** - All data travels safely over the internet  
✓ **One Person, One Vote** - System prevents duplicate voting  
✓ **Complete Records** - Every action is recorded for transparency  
✓ **Permission-Based Access** - Users only see what they're allowed to  
✓ **Anonymous Voting** - Your vote remains secret  
✓ **Protected Storage** - Data is safeguarded even if system is compromised

## User Experience Flow

**Step 1:** Student opens the MWECAU election system  
**Step 2:** Enters their university registration number  
**Step 3:** System automatically finds and displays their information  
**Step 4:** Student confirms their personal details are correct  
**Step 5:** Selects their residential area (KIFUMBU, MAWELA, KWACHANGE, WHITE HOUSE, MOSHI MJINI, or ON-CAMPUS)  
**Step 6:** Provides email address for system notifications  
**Step 7:** Access granted - voting dashboard opens  
**Step 8:** Views all available elections they can participate in  
**Step 9:** Makes their voting choices  
**Step 10:** Receives confirmation that vote was recorded  
**Step 11:** Can view results after election period ends

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I vote?
Simply visit the MWECAU election system website, enter your registration number, verify your details, and make your selections. The entire process takes less than 5 minutes.

### Is my vote secret?
Yes, absolutely. The system is designed so that no one can see how you voted. Your vote is completely anonymous and private.

### What if I'm studying abroad?
No problem! As long as you have internet access, you can vote from anywhere in the world using any device.

### Can I change my vote after submitting?
No, once you submit your vote, it's final. This ensures the integrity of the election process.

### What if I encounter technical problems?
Support will be available during all election periods to help with any technical issues you might experience.

### What if I'm unsure about my residential area or course code?
The system will help you identify your correct residential area and course during the registration process based on your student records.
